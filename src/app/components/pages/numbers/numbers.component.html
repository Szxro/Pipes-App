Decimal Pipe and Percentage Pipe

Pipes include in Angular for Numbers

  • pi | number
  • pi | number:'4.1-5'
  • pi | currency:'$'
  • pi | currency:'USD':'symbol-narrow':'1.0-4'
  • {{pi | number}}
  • {{pi | number:'4.1-5'}}
  • {{pi | currency:'$'}}
  • {{pi | currency:'USD':'symbol-narrow':'1.0-4'}}
  • por | percent
  • por | percent:'2.2-2'
  • {{por | percent}}
  • {{por | percent:'2.2-2'}}
